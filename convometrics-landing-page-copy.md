## FAQ Section

### **Q: How is this different from Mixpanel or Amplitude?**
**A**: Traditional analytics track clicks and sessions. They'll tell you a conversation "completed" based on length or final message. We read the actual content to determine if the user's problem was really solved. Mixpanel says 74% success. We tell you the real number.

### **Q: What if we already have custom analytics?**
**A**: Your engineering team is brilliant - let them build your product, not your analytics. We've spent 8 months analyzing 150k+ conversations to perfect our algorithms. You'd need 3-6 months and $50k+ in AI costs to build this internally.

### **Q: How accurate is the analysis?**
**A**: Our intent classification achieves 94% accuracy vs. human labelers. More importantly, we identify patterns that humans miss in manual review - like polite churners who seem satisfied but churn within days.

### **Q: Do you support real-time analysis?**
**A**: Yes. Upload historical data for baseline insights, then stream live conversations via API for real-time pattern detection and alerting.

### **Q: What about data privacy?**
**A**: We analyze conversation patterns, not personal information. All analysis happens in your region. We never store conversation content - only derived insights and classifications.

### **Q: Which AI platforms do you support?**
**A**: Any platform that can export conversation data. Popular integrations: ChatGPT API logs, Claude conversations, custom chat applications, Intercom, Zendesk, and more.
