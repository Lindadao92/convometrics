# Convometrics Customer Research Framework

## TARGET CUSTOMER INTERVIEW SCRIPT

### **Pre-Call Setup**
**Ideal Interviewee Profile**:
- Product Manager or Head of Product at AI/LLM company
- Building consumer-facing AI applications  
- Team size: 5-50 engineers
- Has existing analytics setup (Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.)

### **Opening (5 minutes)**
"Hi [Name], thanks for taking time to chat. I'm building analytics tools specifically for AI conversation products, and I'd love to understand your current workflow for understanding user success with your AI features."

**Context Questions**:
1. What does your AI product do? (conversational AI, code generation, etc.)
2. How many conversations/sessions do you handle per month?
3. Who on your team is responsible for understanding AI product performance?

### **Problem Discovery (10 minutes)**

**Current State Questions**:
1. "How do you currently measure whether your AI conversations are successful?"
2. "When users have a bad experience with your AI, how do you find out about it?"
3. "What's your process for identifying which AI interactions are failing and why?"
4. "How often do you review AI conversation data, and what tools do you use?"

**Pain Point Validation**:
5. "Tell me about the last time you discovered a major issue with your AI user experience. How did you find it?"
6. "What percentage of your AI conversations do you think are actually successful from the user's perspective?"
7. "How much time does your team spend manually reviewing conversations to understand problems?"

### **Solution Validation (10 minutes)**

**Concept Introduction**:
"What if you could get a Monday morning email that said: 'Your AI success rate dropped to 52% last week. 14 users had failed experiences that likely led to churn. The biggest issue was connect_api failures - fixing this could recover $3,200 in at-risk revenue.' Would that be valuable?"

**Feature Validation Questions**:
1. "What would be the most valuable insight in that weekly briefing?"
2. "How would you want to receive these insights? (email, Slack, dashboard)"
3. "What would make you prioritize fixing an AI conversation issue?"
4. "How important is connecting conversation failures to business metrics like churn or revenue?"

### **Competitive Landscape (5 minutes)**
1. "What tools do you currently use to understand user behavior?" 
2. "Have you tried any LLM-specific analytics tools? (LangSmith, Humanloop, etc.)"
3. "What's missing from your current analytics setup for AI products?"

### **Closing (5 minutes)**
1. "If this tool existed tomorrow, what would you be willing to pay for it monthly?"
2. "Who else on your team would need to see these insights?"
3. "Would you be interested in being a design partner as we build this?"

## CUSTOMER PERSONA RESEARCH

### **Primary Persona: AI Product Manager**

**Demographics**:
- Title: Product Manager, Senior PM, Head of Product
- Company: AI startup (Series A-C), or AI team at larger company
- Team: 10-100 person company, 3-15 person product team
- Location: SF Bay Area, NYC, remote

**Goals**:
- Ship AI features that users actually love
- Understand why AI interactions fail or succeed
- Prove ROI of AI product investments to leadership
- Reduce user churn from bad AI experiences

**Pain Points**:
- Can't tell if AI conversations are working without manual review
- Traditional analytics (sessions, clicks) don't capture AI UX quality
- Spends hours reading conversation logs to find patterns
- Leadership asks "how's the AI performing?" and they can't answer quickly

**Day in the Life**:
- Checks Mixpanel/Amplitude for user metrics
- Spot-checks conversation logs when issues are reported
- Attends engineering standups discussing AI model performance
- Reports to leadership on product KPIs and user feedback

### **Secondary Persona: AI Engineering Lead**

**Demographics**:
- Title: Staff Engineer, Engineering Manager, CTO
- Background: ML/AI engineering, full-stack with AI focus
- Company: Same as PM persona

**Goals**:
- Build reliable AI features with good user experience
- Debug and improve AI model performance
- Optimize conversation flows for better success rates
- Balance model accuracy with user satisfaction

**Pain Points**:
- Model metrics (accuracy, latency) don't translate to user happiness
- Hard to identify which conversation patterns cause user frustration
- Debugging failed conversations is time-consuming
- Needs data to prioritize AI improvement projects

- **3+ companies** willing to be design partners

2. What's the current manual process and how much time does it take?

4. What's the optimal price point and packaging?
