# Convometrics Content Marketing Strategy

## Content Mission
**"Educate AI product teams that completion rates lie, engagement theater kills products, and real user satisfaction requires deeper conversation analysis."**

---

## Core Content Pillars

### 1. **"Beyond Vanity Metrics"** (Technical Education)
**Audience:** AI Product Managers, Data Scientists, ML Engineers
**Goal:** Position Convometrics as the alternative to misleading engagement metrics

**Content Types:**
- **Blog Posts:** "Why Your AI's 90% Completion Rate is Actually 40% User Satisfaction"
- **Case Studies:** "How [Company] Discovered Their 'High-Engagement' Sessions Were Actually User Frustration Loops"
- **Technical Guides:** "The AI Product Manager's Guide to Real Conversation Analysis"
- **Comparison Posts:** "Completion Rate vs. Intent Success Rate: What's the Difference?"

**Key Messages:**
- High engagement ≠ happy users
- Completion rate ≠ problem solved
- Traditional metrics create blind spots that kill products
- Real user satisfaction requires intent-level analysis

### 2. **"Hidden Patterns in AI Conversations"** (Insight-Driven)
**Audience:** AI Product Teams, Customer Success, Support Leaders
**Goal:** Showcase unique insights only conversation analysis can reveal

**Content Types:**
- **Pattern Reports:** "The Polite Churner: Why 'OK Thanks' is a Red Flag"
- **Data Stories:** "We Analyzed 146K AI Conversations. Here's What We Found."
- **Infographics:** Visual patterns in conversation data
- **Video Demos:** Screen recordings showing pattern detection in action

**Key Messages:**
- Conversations contain signals that surface metrics miss
- Patterns repeat across platforms and use cases
- Early detection prevents churn and improves satisfaction
- AI teams need conversation archaeology, not just engagement tracking

### 3. **"AI Product Management Reality Check"** (Industry Commentary)
**Audience:** AI Product Leaders, CTOs, Startup Founders
**Goal:** Establish Linda as thought leader in AI product evaluation

**Content Types:**
- **Opinion Pieces:** "Why Most AI Startups Are Measuring Success Wrong"
- **Industry Analysis:** "The State of AI Product Metrics in 2026"
- **Framework Posts:** "A Better Framework for Evaluating AI Product Success"
- **Prediction Posts:** "What AI Product Metrics Will Matter in 2027"

**Key Messages:**
- Current AI metrics landscape is broken
- Smart teams are moving beyond vanity metrics
- User satisfaction prediction requires deeper analysis
- Convometrics represents the future of AI product evaluation

## Content Distribution Strategy

### **1. Owned Channels**

#### **Convometrics Blog** (Primary Hub)
- **Publishing Frequency:** 2 posts/week
- **Content Mix:** 40% Technical, 35% Insight-Driven, 25% Commentary
- **SEO Focus:** "AI conversation analysis", "AI product metrics", "chatbot analytics"
- **Lead Magnets:** Free conversation analysis reports, metrics framework guides

#### **LinkedIn** (Professional Network)
- **Linda's Personal Profile:** Thought leadership, industry commentary
- **Company Page:** Product updates, case studies, pattern insights
- **Post Frequency:** Daily (mix of original + blog amplification)
- **Engagement Strategy:** Comment on AI product posts, share insights on industry discussions

#### **X/Twitter** (Real-Time Insights)
- **Content Focus:** Quick insights, pattern observations, industry hot takes  
- **Thread Strategy:** Break down complex insights into digestible threads
- **Engagement:** Respond to AI product team discussions, share data findings

### **2. Earned Channels**

- **Target Publications:** First Round Review, HBR, TechCrunch, VentureBeat
- **Podcast Appearances:** AI product management shows, startup podcasts
- **Conference Speaking:** AI conferences, product management events
- **Industry Roundtables:** Expert panels on AI metrics and evaluation

#### **PR Strategy**
- **Data-Driven PR:** Release quarterly "State of AI Conversations" reports
- **Pattern Announcements:** PR around significant pattern discoveries
- **Industry Research:** Partner with universities on conversation analysis research

### **3. Paid Amplification**

#### **LinkedIn Ads** (B2B Focus)

#### **Industry Newsletter Sponsorships**
- **Target:** AI/ML newsletters, product management publications

## Content Production System

### **Content Calendar Structure**

#### **Monday:** Technical Education
- Deep-dive blog posts on conversation analysis methodology
- How-to guides for AI product managers
- Technical comparison posts (metrics frameworks, analysis approaches)

#### **Tuesday:** Pattern Insights  
- New pattern discoveries from conversation data
- Case studies showing pattern impact on business outcomes
- Visual data stories and infographics

#### **Wednesday:** Industry Commentary
- Reaction to AI industry news through conversation analysis lens
- Opinion pieces on AI product management trends
- Framework and strategy posts

#### **Thursday:** Product/Demo Content
- Convometrics feature highlights and use cases
- Customer success stories and testimonials
- Demo videos showing analysis in action

#### **Friday:** Community & Amplification
- Curate and comment on industry discussions
- Amplify the week's content across all channels
- Engage with AI product community conversations

### **Content Creation Workflow**

1. **Research & Data Mining (Weekly)**
   - Analyze conversation patterns for content inspiration
   - Monitor industry discussions and pain points
   - Track competitor content and messaging gaps

2. **Content Creation (Batch Production)**
   - Write 2 weeks of blog content in advance
   - Create visual assets and infographics weekly  
   - Develop LinkedIn post templates for consistent messaging

3. **Distribution & Amplification**
   - Schedule content across all platforms
   - Engage actively in the first 2 hours post-publish
   - Repurpose long-form content into multiple formats

4. **Performance Analysis (Monthly)**
   - Track content performance across channels
   - Analyze lead generation from each content type
   - Optimize high-performing formats and topics
